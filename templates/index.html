Famous Cardiologists

Dr. Vivek Jawali

Dr. Vivek Jawali is a well-known Cardiologist, who has an experience of more than 40 years He specializes in laparoscopic surgery, cardiac ablation, cardiac catheterization, carotid angioplasty & stenting, coronary angiogram, heart valve replacement, device closure, and several other procedures. He has performed more than 18,000 cardiothoracic & vascular surgeries so far. He also performed India’s first beating heart bypass surgery in 1992, and India's first minimally invasive bypass surgery in 1994.

Dr. Robert Coelho

Dr. Robert is a Paediatric Cardiac Surgeon of high acclaim, practicing successfully for more than 21+ years. Dr. Robert Coelho holds the record of performing 6100+ surgeries treating children with congenital and rheumatic heart diseases across 30 countries. Dr. Coelho is also known for the first successful Norwood operation in India in 2003, as well as performing the country’s first successful Nikaidoh procedure for TGA / VSD / PS in 2005.

Dr. Ajay Kaul

Dr. Ajay Kaul is one of India’s most skilled and experienced cardiac bypass surgeons and considered among the top 10 cardiologists in India. He has 38 years of expertise and has performed over 12,000 cardiac surgeries, including 4600 total arteriolar coronary bypass surgeries. Dr. Ajay Kaul graduated from Bombay University with a master’s degree in cardiothoracic and vascular surgery.
